About Nick

London's Premier
Live Caricaturist

For over two decades, Nick Bronowski has been the caricaturist of choice for London's most exclusive events. With a rare combination of technical mastery, lightning speed, and genuine warmth, Nick transforms every guest into a work of art — and every event into a memorable occasion.

Whether capturing celebrities backstage at Wembley Arena, entertaining crowds at charity galas, or delivering bespoke commissions to clients across the globe, Nick's versatility and professionalism are unmatched.

His style adapts to any brief: from flattering portraits to bold caricatures, traditional pencil to vibrant watercolour — every piece is a keepsake that guests take home and treasure for years.
