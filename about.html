Biography

A Life Drawn in Character

Nick Bronowski has spent over two decades establishing himself as one of London's most sought-after caricaturists and multimedia artists. With a portfolio that spans intimate private commissions, high-profile celebrity encounters, and large-scale corporate events, Nick brings a rare blend of artistic precision and genuine entertainment value to every performance.

His live caricature sessions are renowned for their warmth and energy. Working face-to-face with guests — from Charlotte Hawkins at charity galas to Jon Snow at political awards ceremonies — Nick has an extraordinary ability to put subjects at ease while capturing their character with speed, humour, and uncanny accuracy.

Beyond live performance, Nick's studio commissions demonstrate a remarkable versatility: intricate pencil portraits delivered backstage to rock legends like Tommy Lee of Mötley Crüe; vivid Beatles-themed watercolours for global logistics giant Rock-It Logistics; intimate family pieces for clients in Switzerland, Italy, and the USA.

Each piece Nick creates — whether drawn in minutes at a gala or developed over weeks in the studio — is a bespoke work of art that captures not just a likeness, but a personality. It is this commitment to genuine artistry that keeps clients returning and recommending him worldwide.
