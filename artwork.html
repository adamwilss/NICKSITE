Artistic Approach

How Nick Works

✏️
Traditional & Digital Techniques

Equally at home with pencil on paper or a digital stylus — Nick's command of both mediums means every commission can be perfectly tailored to its purpose and presentation.

🎯
Custom Commissions

Every studio commission is developed in close collaboration with the client. Nick takes time to understand what makes the subject unique — and builds that into every mark on the page.

📦
Keepsake Presentation

Live caricatures are presented in acetate sleeves; studio commissions are carefully packaged and shipped worldwide. Every piece leaves Nick's hands ready to be treasured.
