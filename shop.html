Shop & Commission FAQs

Simply use the form below to describe your idea — subject, style, size, occasion, and any specific details you'd like incorporated. Nick will respond within 24 hours to discuss and provide a quote.

Yes — Nick ships artwork worldwide using tracked, insured couriers. Original pieces are professionally packaged to ensure safe delivery. Shipping costs are calculated at point of sale.

Bank transfer, PayPal, and major credit/debit cards are all accepted. A 50% deposit is required to commence a commission, with the balance due on completion.

Prints can be returned within 14 days if undamaged. Bespoke commissions are non-refundable once work has begun, though Nick will work with you to ensure complete satisfaction before delivery.

Pencil sketches typically take 1–2 weeks; detailed watercolour commissions 2–4 weeks. Digital commissions are usually completed within 5–7 working days. Rush commissions can often be accommodated.

Yes — framing options are available on request for both prints and original artworks. Please mention your framing preference when placing your order and Nick will provide options and pricing.
